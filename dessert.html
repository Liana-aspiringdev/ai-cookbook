Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that's known for its rich, creamy texture and layers of flavor. The name tiramisu means "pick me up" or "cheer me up" in Italian, which refers to its energizing ingredients like coffee and sugar.

Ingredients for tiramisu

  • Espresso or strong coffee
  • Golden Rum or use vanilla extract
  • Ladyfingers or sponge cake cookies
  • Egg yolks and sugar
  • Mascarpone cheese or use full-fat cream cheese
  • Heavy Whipping Cream - preferrably chilled right out of the fridge
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder - optional, for garnish
